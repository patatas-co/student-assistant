Purpose & Objectives

Transform the manual student assistant program into a transparent, efficient, and data-driven experience for both students and administrators.

The Challenge

Existing application and support workflows rely on paper forms, siloed communication, and fragmented monitoring. This often leads to delayed placements, incomplete documentation, and limited visibility of student assistant support outcomes.

The Solution

Our web-based system digitizes end-to-end processes—allowing students to apply, get matched, and receive support seamlessly while administrators orchestrate approvals, documentation, and analytics in one place.

  • Centralized Application Portal
  • Real-time Status Tracking
  • Support Services Directory
  • Performance Dashboards

Streamlined Workflow

Every stakeholder stays informed with guided steps, notifications, and real-time status visibility.

01

Application Launch

Students create profiles, submit requirements, and choose desired placements or support services.

02

Validation & Assessment

Coordinators verify eligibility, schedule interviews, and evaluate qualifications via built-in rubrics.

03

Placement & Onboarding

Approved students receive assignment notifications, digital contracts, and onboarding checklists.

04

Support Delivery

Support offices log assistance rendered, monitor compliance, and handle escalations collaboratively.

05

Insights & Reporting

Dashboards present KPIs on placements, workloads, fulfillment, and student satisfaction trends.
